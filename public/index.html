        Prototype privacy architecture

        Useful context. Explicit boundaries.

        • Personal workspaces are stored in this browser’s local storage.
        • Screenshot OCR runs in the browser without a paid AI request.
        • Retained source images use a separate local evidence store.
        • The prototype is not encrypted; avoid highly sensitive information.
        • Paid API calls and voice transcription remain disabled.
        • Context is retrieved through exact person and situation IDs.
        • AI interpretations remain unreviewed until you explicitly respond.

        Production relationship memory would require authentication, encryption, deletion controls, and a formal privacy review.
