## Short description

Second Mind is a user-controlled cognitive companion that connects people,
messages, events, notes, and screenshots in an evidence-linked relationship
graph and timeline. It separates observation from interpretation, exposes
uncertainty, tests competing explanations, and rehearses how a response might
be interpreted—without deciding intent or replacing human judgment.

## Final differentiation paragraph

Most AI products optimize for generating language. Second Mind first constructs
an inspectable model of the relevant people, events, commitments, evidence, and
uncertainty. Its five reasoning lenses hold reported facts constant while
changing the cognitive operation applied to them, and its Communication Studio
uses that selected context across Draft, Reply, Review, Rewrite, Predict, and
Compare without overwriting the user's original. This makes epistemic discipline
a testable product behavior rather than a promise hidden in a system prompt.
